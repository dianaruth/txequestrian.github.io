Membership Requirements

  • Currently enrolled UT undergrad student; to compete at shows, members must be enrolled in at least 12 hours.
  • Pay all IHSA and Team Dues
  • Maintain a 2.25 GPA or higher
  • Provide documentation of health insurance (required by IHSA and UT)
  • Meet lesson and team criteria to compete

Time Commitment

The time commitment for the team is a perfect balance between work and play, providing team members with several opportunities to ride, while still maintaining plenty of time for their studies.


Lessons

Team members are divided into lesson groups and take weekly lessons with the Coach. Beginner riders may need to take private lessons before joining a group. If the Coach must cancel a lesson due to an emergency or unsafe weather conditions, the lesson will be rescheduled for a later date in the semester. If a rider misses their lesson, they are responsible to make up this lesson on their own. Make-up lesson weeks are provided at various times during the year. Open lesson spots due to rider cancellations can also be taken by a rider who needs to make up a lesson. Lessons are held at Idylwood Stables.
